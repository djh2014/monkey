Our Mission

Do what you are great at and leave your wallet home. Through our platform you earn Badgers, which are 15 minute units of time by helping out others. You can then spend your Badgers on anything that would make you a more productive, happier, and overall better person.

About Us

Why not leverage your skills and strenghts in order to imrove areas you are weak in? What if you could help others with your talent but get something in return. What if you could eliminate the need for cash and instead trade talents? However, instead of a direct trade between two people, you accumulate virtual currency (Badgers) by helping others and you can spend that currency (Badgers) anyway you want. No matching skill sets or give and return. The currency is based on time and the more time you put in, the more you get out.
